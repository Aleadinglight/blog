Try to apply what I learned from my working time in an enterprise company.

## Incident
### What happen?
- When and what which services?
- Who who were experiencing the incident? (which customers, are they strategic customers or paygo user? How many of them? Did the customer report it to us, or we figure it out first hand?)
- What was the impact for the customer? (our services were unavailable so what did the customer experience?)
- What was the reason for that impact?

### What was our response?
Answer these with timeline
- When did we notice the problem?
- What actions did we take? 
### 5 Why?
### What did we learn?
For example:
- Why did it take us 2 hours to respond?
- Should we update our playbook?

### How can we improve from this moving on?
Create an action items list. Make sure to specify the priority: critical, important, improvement,...
